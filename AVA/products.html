The ultimate way to get the beach the AVA Dune Glider is stable and easy to maneuver well beyond it’s 330 pound weight limit. The cart is constructed of a rust resistant, lightweight, marine grade aluminum, while the 20 inch tires ride on fiberglass-reinforced nylon wheels for excellent balance, even over rough terrain. This cart will change your beach experience.
